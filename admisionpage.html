Admission Process

The admission process at Maharshi Vidhay Mandir Khargone is designed to be straightforward and transparent. Follow these steps to apply for admission:

  1. Obtain the admission form from the school office or download it from our website.
  2. Fill out the form with the required details and attach the necessary documents.
  3. Submit the completed form to the school office by the specified deadline.
  4. Attend the scheduled interview and assessment sessions.
  5. Receive the admission decision and complete the enrollment process.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for admission to Maharshi Vidhay Mandir Khargone, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Age criteria as per the class applied for.
  • Previous academic records and performance.
  • Successful completion of the admission assessment and interview.
  • Submission of all required documents.

Important Dates

Keep the following important dates in mind during the admission process:

  • Application Form Availability: May 1, 2024
  • Submission Deadline: May 31, 2024
  • Interview and Assessment: June 10 - June 20, 2024
  • Admission Decision Notification: July 1, 2024
