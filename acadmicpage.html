Academic Programs

At Maharshi Vidhay Mandir Khargone, we offer a range of academic programs designed to meet the diverse needs and interests of our students. Our programs are structured to provide a strong foundation in core subjects while encouraging exploration and innovation.

  • Primary Education (Grades 1-5)
  • Middle School (Grades 6-8)
  • Secondary Education (Grades 9-10)
  • Senior Secondary Education (Grades 11-12) with Science, Commerce, and Arts streams

Our Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a love for learning. We follow the guidelines set by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and incorporate modern teaching methods and technology to enhance the learning experience.

The curriculum includes:

  • Core subjects: Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, English, and Hindi
  • Electives: Computer Science, Physical Education, Fine Arts, and more
  • Extracurricular activities: Sports, Music, Dance, Drama, and various clubs
  • Life skills and value education programs
